Usage

The usage is pretty simple. Follow these steps:

  • Select your analyzer in the list of available drivers
  • Select the correct port in the list of available ports
  • Press the TEST button.
  • When the status message es printed in green, press the UPDATE button and you're ready to work.
  • When the status message is printed in red, select another port in the list of available ports and again press the UPDATE button. If this fails again, please check your analyzer connection to your computer.
